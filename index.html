What's CVA?

Creator Venture Accelerator is an early-stage holding company that helps top talent and creators design, fund, and scale companies around their influence.

CVA is a group that wants visionary & visible people to make great things. (High Quality)

Nobody is just a founder, creator, investor, or operator. (Dynamic)

So, we back you to do it all.

We provide the frameworks, funding, network and tools to make that easier, better, and faster.

We are:

  • – Not an agency
  • – Not a talent manager
  • – Not a VC fund

We're a business partner. Influence is capital. We show you how to compound it.

Why Creators?

Emily Herrera

"Innovation is all about growth. Let's just find a sector that's growing like crazy..." – Ron Conway, 2012

CVAccelerator began with a simple question: What if the next generation of founders comes from YouTube, TikTok, and Substack?

For years, creators have built loyal audiences while being excluded from startup infrastructure. They've been treated as entertainers, not entrepreneurs.

We saw something different. We saw creators as the next consumer ecosystem. Not just personal brands – personal economies.

So we built the infrastructure they were missing. We give creators the structure, support, and capital they need to build enduring companies.

The next 1,000 unicorns start on YouTube.
